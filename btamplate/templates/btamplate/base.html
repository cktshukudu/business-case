Motivation of this App.

This project was motivated by noticing that most SMEs are struggling and they do not have a proper structure or templates of some sort to follow. Some businesses do create good business cases but half way through the project they fail to stick to it due to changes that happen as the project is progressing. Some stop going back to the business case as soon as they start gaining the benefits even though the project is not fully complete and that leads to the project not being a success at the end of the day.

Objectives

We want SMEs to have a clear, ongoing basis for determining whether the project is worth continuing or not.
